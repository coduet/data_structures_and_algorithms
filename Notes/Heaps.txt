Heaps
-----

Heaps are Complete binary tree. Usually represented with arrays.

Types
	i. Min heap
		- for each node in a min heap, data of the node is less than the data of its left and right child.
		- root of the heap is minimum.

	ii. Max heap
		- for each node in a max heap, data of the node is greater than the data of its left and right child.
		- root of the heap is maximum.
